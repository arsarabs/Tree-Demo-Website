This is a follow-up to a previous SEO optimization pass on a Next.js/Tailwind junk removal website (The Junk Moose, demo.launchedops.com). The homepage is already optimized. Now expand the site into a full multi-page architecture to dramatically increase organic keyword surface area and local SEO authority.

Do NOT hardcode any API keys. Do NOT put any secrets in client-side code.

---

## GOAL
Transform this single-page site into a multi-page Next.js app with dedicated routes for services, locations, and supporting pages. Each page gets its own metadata, schema, and keyword targeting.

---

## PAGE ARCHITECTURE TO BUILD

### 1. Service Pages — `/services/[slug]`

Create a dynamic route at `/app/services/[slug]/page.tsx`.

Build individual pages for each service:
- `/services/furniture-appliance-removal`
- `/services/construction-debris-removal`
- `/services/yard-waste-removal`
- `/services/full-property-cleanouts`
- `/services/commercial-junk-removal`
- `/services/same-day-junk-removal`

Each service page must include:
- Unique `<title>` and `<meta description>` targeting that service + "Portland OR"
- H1 with the service name + location (e.g. "Furniture & Appliance Removal in Portland OR")
- 150-200 words of unique body copy describing the service, what's included, and why locals choose The Junk Moose
- A relevant FAQ section (3-5 questions specific to that service) with FAQPage schema
- Service schema (matching what was built in the SEO pass)
- A sticky CTA ("Get a Free Quote") throughout
- Breadcrumb nav: Home > Services > [Service Name] with BreadcrumbList schema
- Link back to homepage and to the locations hub

Build a `/services` index page at `/app/services/page.tsx` that:
- Lists all 6 services as cards with links
- Has its own title/meta: "Junk Removal Services Portland OR | The Junk Moose"
- Includes a short intro paragraph

## INTERNAL LINKING STRATEGY

- Homepage should link to all service pages and the locations hub
- Every service page links to all location pages ("We offer [service] in [city], [city], and [city]")
- Every location page links to all service pages
- About, Reviews, Contact all link back to homepage and /contact
- This creates a full internal link mesh — critical for passing PageRank across the site
